# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Extension Name

EpochExt

## Short Description

Hover over Unix timestamps on any website to see readable dates in your chosen timezone, including Eastern EDT/EST.

## Detailed Description

EpochExt makes Unix epoch timestamps readable while you browse.

Hover over timestamp text on any website and EpochExt shows a clean tooltip with the converted date, time, and timezone abbreviation. It works with common timestamp precisions, including seconds, milliseconds, microseconds, and nanoseconds.

By default, EpochExt displays US Eastern time and automatically handles EDT and EST. You can also choose Central, Mountain, Pacific, UTC, London, Central Europe, India, China, Japan, or your browser's current timezone from the toolbar popup.

Features:

- Hover to convert Unix timestamps instantly
- Supports 10-digit, 13-digit, 16-digit, and 19-digit timestamps
- Choose your preferred display timezone
- Optional click-to-pin tooltip mode
- Sensible timestamp filtering to reduce false matches
- Works locally in your browser

Privacy:

EpochExt does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. Timestamp detection and conversion happen locally in your browser. The extension uses Chrome storage only to save your timezone and tooltip preferences.

Permissions:

EpochExt requests the storage permission so it can remember your settings. It runs on web pages so it can detect timestamp text and show the conversion tooltip.
